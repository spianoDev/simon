Welcome to Simon!

To play, hit the small red button under simon. Simon will play one sound. You must copy what he does and hit the Simon button to enter your response. If correct, Simon will add a new note to the sequence of notes. Continue these steps to win!

If you are correct, your score will increase and you will hear a pleasant xylophone sound. If you make a mistake, you will hear a buzzer and your score returns to 0.
